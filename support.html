Frequently asked

Why does a stop say "No buses scheduled"?
BusPulse shows buses that are being tracked live right now. A stop can be empty late in the evening or on less frequent routes when no bus is currently running toward it. Pull down to refresh.

Do I need an account?
No. Open the app and go — no sign-up, no login.

How do I get a notification before my bus?
Open a stop and tap the bell next to an arrival. BusPulse will alert you a few minutes before it's due.

Why is location needed?
Your location is used only on your device to show nearby stops and walk times. It is never stored or shared.
