WhereIsMyBus — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

WhereIsMyBus ("the app") is a real-time TransLink transit app for Metro Vancouver. This policy explains what information the app accesses and how it is used.

Location Data

The app requests access to your device's location while the app is in use. Your location is used solely to show nearby bus stops and estimate your walk time to stops. Your location is never stored, transmitted to any server, or shared with any third party. All location processing happens on your device.

Transit Data

The app fetches real-time arrival and vehicle data from TransLink's public GTFS-RT feed. These requests do not include any personal information. No user data is sent to TransLink or any other party.

Notifications

If you choose to enable departure notifications, the app schedules local notifications on your device. No notification data is sent to any server. You can disable notifications at any time in your device Settings.

Favorites and Recent Stops

Stop favorites and recently viewed stops are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Analytics and Tracking

The app collects no analytics, crash reports, or usage data. There is no tracking of any kind.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Apple Maps (provided by Apple) for map display. Apple's own privacy policy governs Apple Maps data. The app does not use any advertising SDKs or analytics platforms.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
